Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

Remedy Point Solutions, Inc. ("we", "us", "our") operates the Pamyat mobile application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account information: Phone number (for authentication via SMS verification), display name you provide during setup.

Memorial data: Names, dates of birth and death, cemetery names, grave locations (GPS coordinates), photos, inscriptions, and plot information you enter when creating memorials.

Usage data: We may collect anonymous analytics such as screens visited and feature usage to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and provide access to the app
  • To store and display the memorials you create
  • To enable sharing memorials with family members you invite
  • To send you SMS verification codes during sign-in
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure storage.
